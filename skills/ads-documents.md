# ADS Documents — Analytics Dojo SMC Private

Covers drafting employment offer letters, employment agreements, internship offer letters, and internship agreements for **Analytics Dojo SMC Private**, a Private Limited company incorporated under the laws of Pakistan. All documents are governed by Pakistani law and use locked company identity and pre-approved clause language. All output is for attorney review only and does not constitute legal advice.

**Jurisdiction:** Pakistan — Income Tax Ordinance 2001; Industrial and Commercial Employment (Standing Orders) Ordinance 1968; Factories Act 1934; Employees' Old-Age Benefits Act 1976; Maternity and Paternity Leaves Act 2020; Personal Data Protection Bill 2022.

**Compliance source:** All figures and policy positions in this file are sourced from the ADS Labour Laws Compliance Audit 2025. The resolution rule is: **Revisit Dec 2025 column first → Revisit June 2025 column if Dec 2025 is blank → ADS Policy column if both revisit columns are blank.** Where the Dec 2025 column says "No Change" or "no change", the ADS Policy column value stands as current.

**Use this reference for Pipeline G only.** Do NOT apply DSD standards, Washington-law defaults, AAA arbitration, or OFAC representations to these documents. Those belong to Pipeline A.

## Step 1: Doc-Type Identification — REQUIRED FIRST. Never draft without confirming this.

Confirm two things before any other step:

| Question | Options |
|----------|---------|
| **Document type** | Standard Employment / Sales Employment / Business Development Employment / Internship |
| **Output format** | Offer Letter only / Agreement only / Both (Letter + Agreement) |

**Type definitions:**

- **Standard Employment** — general, technical, or operational roles with fixed salary only. No commission exhibit.
- **Sales Employment** — sales roles. Adds Exhibit B: Variable Compensation with fixed commission rate + quota-based commission on exceeding target.
- **Business Development Employment** — BD roles. Adds Exhibit B: Variable Compensation with standard commission rate + enhanced rate for deals exceeding a defined threshold percentage of quarterly target. Formula differs from Sales — do not substitute one for the other.
- **Internship** — internship roles only. Stipend not salary. 15-day mutual termination notice (not 30). Benefits unlock in two stages: from day one (internet + food allowances) and from month six (health/life insurance, OPD, laptop, office setup allowance, L&D). No PF, no EOBI, no annual leave entitlements during internship.

## Step 5: Document Architecture

### Offer Letter Structure (all types)

Offer letters are shorter commercial documents. They set the headline terms only. The full binding obligations live in the Agreement.

```
1. Header block: Date | Recipient Name | Email
2. Salutation and opening: offer statement; company name; purpose of letter
3. Questions contact line: HR contact name + direct contact
4. Terms (bullet list):
     a. Effective start date
     b. Background verification contingency
     c. Job title and reporting line
     d. Base compensation and payroll frequency
     e. Incentive bonus eligibility (discretionary; CEO determination)
     f. Employee Handbook acknowledgment
     g. NDA requirement
     h. Probation period: duration, notice (5 days each side), confirmation process
     i. Probation task completion requirement
     j. Benefits during probation (see benefits schedule)
     k. Benefits after confirmation (see benefits schedule) — Standard/Sales/BD only
     l. Internship benefits by stage (day-one and 6-month) — Internship only
     m. Company laptop acknowledgment + damage/loss liability
     n. Entire agreement / supersession clause
     o. Termination notice: 30 days confirmed (employment) / 15 days (internship)
5. Acceptance language; offer effective for five business days
6. Signature block: Company (on behalf of Analytics Dojo SMC Private) + Recipient
7. Exhibit B — Variable Compensation (Sales and BD only; see Exhibit formats below)
8. Footer: Address | Phone | Email
```

### Agreement Structure (all types)

Agreements are the full binding contract. They incorporate and expand on the offer letter terms.

```
1. Title block: ANALYTICS DOJO SMC PRIVATE [TYPE] AGREEMENT
2. Parties clause: Company + Employee/Intern + Client (Data Science Dojo LLC) + Effective Date
3. Employment / Internship section:
     a. Position
     b. Background Verification and Offer Contingency
     c. Duties
     d. Company Policies
     e. Employee / Intern Behavior (including religious apparel carve-out; non-discriminatory application)
     f. Travel (domestic: prior intimation to Team Lead; international: 15 working days advance approval)
     g. Stable Internet obligation (primary + secondary backup connection)
     h. Power Backup obligation
     i. Location Tracking consent (company laptop; working hours; no prior notice required)
     j. Assigned Working Hours / attendance sign-in (failure = PTO deduction)
     k. Attendance and Leave procedure (prior approval required; leaves not granted retroactively)
     l. Communication and Responsiveness (reasonable response time; repeated failure = performance issue)
     m. Probation Period (3 months; 5-day notice; confirmation notification at end)
     n. Termination (30 days confirmed / 15 days internship; notice-period service; Termination Date definition)
     o. Term (begins effective date; continues until terminated)
     p. Compensation and Benefits (reference to Exhibit A)
     q. Income Tax (Income Tax Ordinance 2001; withholding at prescribed rates)
     r. Compensation Subject to Change (written consent of both parties required)
     s. Equipment and Amenities

     a. IP Assignment of Inventions and Creations (to Client)

11. Signature block
```

- **Never apply Washington law, AAA arbitration, or DSD standards** to any document in this pipeline. These are Pakistan-law documents only.
- **Never apply Pipeline A's 25-section architecture** to ADS documents. The structure here is different and intentional.
- **PF rate is 6%, not 8.33%.** If an existing document shows 8.33%, flag the discrepancy. Do not use 8.33% in new documents.
- **Health insurance hospitalization limit is PKR 500,000/year**, not PKR 300,000. Use PKR 300,000 for the probation-period health benefit only where that is the template value; use PKR 500,000 for confirmed employment hospitalization coverage.
- **Life insurance is a voluntary ADS benefit**, not a statutory requirement. Never state it as a legal obligation.
- **PTO is 12 working days, sick leave is 5 working days** per the compliance audit. Do not use 18 PTO days or 8 sick days from older templates.
- **Salary minimum floors apply.** Flag any salary below PKR 90,000/month (fixed) or PKR 70,000/month (commission-based) before generating the document.
- **Never invent compensation figures.** If base salary, commission rate, or quota is not provided, mark `[BLANK — confirm X]` and surface it before output.
- **Internship ≠ Employment.** Do not apply EOBI, PF, annual leave entitlements, 30-day notice, death benefit, or offboarding handover to internship documents.
- **Sales ≠ BD commission structure.** Do not substitute one variable compensation exhibit for the other.
- **Sick leave statutory gap.** Always include the counsel flag on sick leave in employment agreements — ADS 5 days is below the Factories Act 1934 statutory minimum of 8 days.
- **Attorney review.** Every output must begin with: *This document is a draft for attorney review only and does not constitute legal advice.*
